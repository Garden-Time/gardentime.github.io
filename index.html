Description

Garden Time is the newest time-management app that rewards you for being on top of your daily schedule. Garden Time imports your to-do lists from other apps, has a personal habits tracker, and allows you to schedule meetings all in one place. Garden Time incorporates gamification. Users can earn points by completing their tasks on time, and use these points to grow plants, unlock plants at new levels, and earn bonus plants by completing challenges.
